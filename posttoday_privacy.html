Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

PostToday ("we", "us") provides an AI tool that helps TikTok creators research content and publish videos to their own TikTok accounts. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we access

  • TikTok account basic info (open ID, display name, avatar) — obtained only after you authorize our app via TikTok Login Kit, and used only to show which account you connected.
  • Content you choose to publish — the video files and captions you explicitly select. We transmit these to TikTok on your behalf via the official Content Posting API.

How we use it

  • To authenticate your TikTok account and display it back to you.
  • To upload and post the specific videos you choose, to your own TikTok profile.

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or post anything without your explicit action.

Data storage & retention

Access tokens are stored securely only for as long as needed to perform actions you request, and are deleted when you disconnect the app. We do not retain your videos after they are delivered to TikTok.

Data deletion

You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok settings, or request full deletion of any data we hold by emailing us. We will delete it within 30 days.

Third parties

We share data only with TikTok, strictly to perform the publishing actions you initiate, in accordance with TikTok's Developer Terms and Policies.
