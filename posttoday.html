PostToday

Research trends & publish to TikTok — in one flow

PostToday is an AI-powered content tool that helps TikTok sellers and creators discover trending products and publish their own videos directly to their TikTok account.

Powered by the TikTok Content Posting API

🔎 Trend research

AI surfaces trending products and content ideas so sellers know what to post next.

✍️ AI captions

Generate platform-optimized captions and hashtags tailored for TikTok.

🚀 Direct publish

Upload and post videos straight to the creator's own TikTok profile — no manual steps.

How it works

1

Connect TikTok — the user authorizes their own account securely via TikTok Login Kit (OAuth). We only access what the user grants.

2

Pick a video & caption — the user selects the video and reviews the AI-generated caption before anything is posted.

3

Publish — the video is uploaded and posted to the user's own TikTok profile through the Content Posting API.

Permissions we request

user.info.basic — to display the connected account name/avatar so the user confirms the correct account.
video.upload / video.publish — to upload and post videos that the user explicitly selects, to their own account only. We never post on behalf of third parties.
